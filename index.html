HACK ACADEMY

ABOUT



WHACK (Wellesley College Hackathon) is Wellesley College's annual hackathon event. We welcome any undergraduate college students ages 18 and above to join us for a weekend of innovation, learning, sharing ideas, and forming new connections!

THEME



This year's theme is Hack Academy! Get ready to revolutionize the way we learn. Inspired by platforms like Quizlet, Duolingo, and Khan Academy, this theme focuses on educational technology (edtech) innovations. Participants are encouraged to develop the next groundbreaking tool that could help students master complex subjects and excel academically. This could be the spark for a new era in education!
